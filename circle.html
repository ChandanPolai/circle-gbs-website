Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about joining and participating in Circle.

What is Circle ?

Circle is a professional networking organization that connects business owners, entrepreneurs, and professionals to help them grow their businesses through referrals, partnerships, and collaborative opportunities.

How do I join a chapter?

Joining is easy! Contact us to find a chapter in your area or explore starting a new one. We'll connect you with local business professionals and help you get started.

What are the membership benefits?

Members enjoy exclusive access to networking events, referral opportunities, business education workshops, mentorship programs, and our online platform.

How much does membership cost?

Membership fees vary by chapter and location. Contact your local chapter for specific pricing information. Most members see ROI within the first few months.

Can I start my own chapter?

Absolutely! We're always looking for passionate business leaders to start new chapters. We provide comprehensive training and ongoing support.
